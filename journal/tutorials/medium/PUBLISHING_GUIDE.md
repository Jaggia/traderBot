# Publishing on Medium: A Quick Guide

## 1. Create Your Account

Go to [medium.com](https://medium.com) and click **"Sign up"**. You can use:

- **Google** (one-click)
- **Apple ID** (one-click)
- **Email** — they send a magic link, no password needed

After signing up, complete your profile: name, bio, photo, and URL slug (e.g., `medium.com/@yourname`).

**Writing and publishing is free.** No payment info required.

Optional: Medium membership ($5/month) lets you read member-only stories and join the Partner Program to earn money from your writing.

---

## 2. Write Your Post

### Start a new story

- Click the **pencil icon** ("Write") in the top-right nav
- Or go directly to **medium.com/new-story**

### Formatting

The editor is block-based. Type text, select it, and use the floating toolbar:

| Format | How |
|---|---|
| **Title** | First large text block (auto-styled as H1) |
| **Subtitle** | Second text block |
| **Headings** | Select text → H2 or H3 |
| **Bold/Italic** | Select text → toolbar |
| **Code blocks** | Type `` ``` `` or use the "+" menu |
| **Blockquotes** | Select text → quote icon, or type `>` |
| **Divider** | Type `---` or `***` on its own line |
| **Lists** | Bulleted or numbered via toolbar |

### Add images

- Click the **"+"** on the left margin of any empty line → image icon
- Or **drag and drop** / **paste from clipboard**
- Supported: JPEG, PNG, GIF
- Click an image to resize or add a caption

### Embed media

Paste a URL (YouTube, Twitter, GitHub gist, CodePen) on its own line and press Enter.

---

## 3. Publish

When your story is ready:

1. Click the green **"Publish"** button (top right)
2. A pre-publish dialog appears:
   - **Tags** — add up to 5. Use all 5 slots — each tag is a separate discovery channel. Prefer specific tags ("Python" over "Programming", "Options Trading" over "Finance")
   - **Newsletter** — optionally send to your subscribers (if enabled)
   - **Visibility** — **Public** (anyone can read) or **Members-only** (required to earn via Partner Program)
   - **Canonical URL** — set this if cross-posting from your own blog (avoids SEO duplicate content penalty)
3. Click **"Publish now"** or **schedule** for a future date/time

---

## 4. After Publishing

Your story gets a unique URL you can share anywhere.

### Manage your stories

Go to **medium.com/me/stories** to see:
- **Drafts** — auto-saved, unpublished
- **Scheduled** — queued for future publication
- **Published** — live
- **Unlisted** — published but only accessible via direct link

You can **edit any published story at any time** — changes are auto-saved.

### Get more reach

**Submit to a publication.** Publications are like magazines on Medium with their own followers. Writing your story first, then using the "..." menu to submit it. The publication editor reviews and accepts or declines. Good for new writers because publications provide an established audience.

**Nominate for Boosting.** Medium's curators review stories and "Boost" high-quality ones for wider distribution. You can self-nominate.

---

## 5. Earning Money (Optional)

Join the **Partner Program** at [medium.com/creators](https://medium.com/creators):

- Must be a Medium member ($5/month)
- Must have published a story in the last 6 months
- Stories must be set to **Members-only**
- Earnings based on: member read time (30+ seconds), engagement (claps, highlights, replies, follows), and boost bonus

The 100-follower requirement has been removed.

## Checklist for Your First Post

- [ ] Clear, specific title (this becomes the SEO title tag)
- [ ] Subtitle (becomes the meta description)
- [ ] Header image (Medium auto-generates the social sharing preview from your first image)
- [ ] Scannable formatting (headers, bold, blockquotes, lists)
- [ ] 5 relevant tags
- [ ] Decide: public or members-only
- [ ] Consider submitting to a relevant publication
- [ ] Share the URL on social media
